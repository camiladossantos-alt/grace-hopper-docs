# Product Overview

## Vision
A calm AI-first platform designed to improve interview confidence through voice-based practice.

## Problem
Users do not have accessible and realistic environments to practice interviews.

## Solution
Voice-based interview simulation with instant AI feedback.

## Core Features
- Voice interviews
- AI feedback
- Speech-to-text
- Progress tracking
- Custom interview questions

## Audience
- Students
- Junior developers
- Career changers
